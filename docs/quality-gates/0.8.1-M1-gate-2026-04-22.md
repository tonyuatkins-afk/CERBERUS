## WARN items

### W14 (WARN, new): DGROUP at 2,016 bytes vs 62 KB soft target

Under target but within the 2 KB hard-reserve window that `tools/dgroup_check.py` defines as the yellow zone. The M1 delta was only +496 bytes, well under its 1,200-byte cap per the 0.8.1 plan — the AT RISK status reflects accumulated pressure from prior milestones rather than an M1 overrun.

M2 (L1 latency + L2 detection) and M3 (DB additions + Hercules variant enum) have tight DGROUP caps (500 + 350 bytes worst case) but may collectively push us past the 62 KB soft target into the hard-reserve window on every metric.

Recommend: before M2 lands, run a CONST-segment audit looking for duplicate string literals and short-but-frequent format strings. The `wmake dgroup-report` tool already exists for this; no new tooling needed.
